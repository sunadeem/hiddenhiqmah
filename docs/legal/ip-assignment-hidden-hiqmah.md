# Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement

> **⚠️ Not legal advice.** This is a plain-English template to get founder-owned IP
> onto the LLC's books. It covers the common case (a solo founder who built
> everything personally before/around forming a single-member LLC). Have a lawyer
> review before signing — especially if there were any other contributors,
> contractors, employers, or open-source obligations. Fill every **[BRACKET]**.

---

**This Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement** (the "Agreement") is made effective
as of **[DATE]** (the "Effective Date"), by and between:

- **[YOUR FULL LEGAL NAME]**, an individual residing at **[YOUR ADDRESS]** (the "Assignor"); and
- **Hidden Hiqmah LLC**, a **[STATE]** limited liability company (the "Company" or "Assignee").

The Assignor and the Company are each a "Party" and together the "Parties."

## 1. Background

1.1. The Assignor conceived, created, and developed the mobile and web application
currently known as **"Hidden Hiqmah"** (the "Product"), together with its source code,
designs, branding, and related materials, prior to and/or in anticipation of forming
the Company.

1.2. The Assignor is the sole owner of all such materials and now wishes to assign all
right, title, and interest in them to the Company, and the Company wishes to accept
that assignment.

## 2. Assigned Intellectual Property

"Assigned IP" means all intellectual property and proprietary rights, worldwide,
whether registered or unregistered, that the Assignor owns and that relate to the
Product, including without limitation:

- **(a) Software** — all source code, object code, scripts, database schemas and
  migrations, configuration, and build tooling for the Product.
- **(b) Names & Brands** — the name "Hidden Hiqmah," any logos, wordmarks, icons, and
  all associated goodwill and trademark rights (registered or common-law).
- **(c) Content & Designs** — all original text, UI/UX designs, graphics, illustrations,
  audio, and other creative works authored by the Assignor for the Product.
- **(d) Domains & Accounts** — all domain names, and the Assignor's rights in any
  developer, hosting, and service accounts used for the Product, to the extent
  transferable.
- **(e) Related Rights** — all copyrights, trademarks, trade secrets, patents and patent
  applications, moral rights, and any other proprietary rights in the foregoing,
  together with all claims for past, present, and future infringement.

For clarity, Assigned IP does **not** include any third-party materials that the Product
merely licenses or embeds (e.g., third-party libraries, licensed Qur'an/hadith text,
reciter audio, or fonts), which remain governed by their own licenses.

## 3. Assignment

3.1. The Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to the Company all
of the Assignor's right, title, and interest in and to the Assigned IP, effective as of
the Effective Date.

3.2. To the extent any Assigned IP cannot be assigned by operation of law (e.g., certain
moral rights), the Assignor hereby waives such rights and/or grants the Company an
exclusive, perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free, sublicensable license to exercise them.

## 4. Consideration

In consideration of this assignment, the Assignor received their membership interest in
the Company and other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of
which is acknowledged. *(For a single-member LLC, the membership interest itself is
customary consideration.)*

## 5. Further Assurances

The Assignor will, at the Company's reasonable request and expense, execute any further
documents and take any further actions reasonably necessary to perfect, record, or
enforce the Company's ownership of the Assigned IP (including trademark and copyright
filings and account/domain transfers).

## 6. Representations & Warranties

The Assignor represents and warrants that: (a) the Assignor is the sole owner of the
Assigned IP; (b) the Assigned IP has not been assigned, licensed exclusively, or
encumbered to any third party; (c) to the Assignor's knowledge, the Assigned IP does not
infringe the rights of any third party; and (d) no other person or entity (employer,
contractor, or collaborator) has a claim to the Assigned IP.

## 7. General

7.1. **Governing law.** This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of **[STATE]**,
without regard to conflict-of-laws principles.

7.2. **Entire agreement.** This Agreement is the entire agreement between the Parties
regarding its subject matter and supersedes all prior discussions.

7.3. **Counterparts / e-signature.** This Agreement may be signed in counterparts and by
electronic signature, each of which is an original.
